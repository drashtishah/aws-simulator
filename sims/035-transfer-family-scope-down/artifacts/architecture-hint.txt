  Per-user scope-down policy pattern (intended):
    Allow s3:ListBucket on bucket
      condition s3:prefix = "customers/${transfer:UserName}/*"
    Allow s3:GetObject/PutObject on
      arn:aws:s3:::.../customers/${transfer:UserName}/*

  Every user is supposed to be isolated to their own folder by
  the scope-down, with the role providing only the maximum
  permissions the scope-down is allowed to grant.
