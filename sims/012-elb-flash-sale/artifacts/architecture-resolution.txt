  PROBLEM 1: ALB NOT PRE-WARMED
  =============================
  ALB scales internal capacity gradually (~2x over 5 min).
  10x spike in 45 seconds exceeded ALB capacity.
  Result: 503 errors, RejectedConnectionCount spike.
  Fix: Contact AWS Support to pre-warm ALB, or use LCU reservation.

  PROBLEM 2: AUTO SCALING TOO SLOW
  =================================
  Policy: CPUUtilization > 70% (simple scaling, 300s cooldown)
  Timeline:
    00:00 - Sale starts, traffic spikes to 2,400 req/s
    00:02 - CPU alarm triggers (2x 60s eval periods)
    00:03 - First instance launched
    00:07 - First instance passes health check
    00:08 - Cooldown expires, second instance launched
  Fix: Add ALBRequestCountPerTarget policy, reduce cooldown to 60s.
